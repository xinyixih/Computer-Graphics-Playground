# 4. Simulation (Physics-Based)  
**Key difference:**  
- *Animation* = artist-driven motion  
- *Simulation* = physically-driven motion  

Subfields:
- Cloth & fabric simulation  
- Smoke / fire / fluids  
- Rigid body dynamics  
- Soft body simulation  
- Hair & fur simulation
